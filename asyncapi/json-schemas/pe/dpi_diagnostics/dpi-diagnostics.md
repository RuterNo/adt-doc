### DpiDiagnostics Message
| Field         | Value                                                       |
|---------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Central Topic | ruter/{operatorId}/{vehicleId}/adt/v3/pe/dpi_diagnostics    |
| Schema        | [ dpi-diagnostics.json ](json-schemas/pe/dpi_diagnostics/dpi-diagnostics.json)|
